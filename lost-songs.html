The story behind this page.

In the summer of 2018 I experienced a hard drive failure on my laptop with two and a half years worth of unfinished songs. Luckily, I had backed up my entire Ableton project folder to my google drive in March. However, I still lost a whole summer worth of work and a couple of songs that I really cared about. Anyway, over the years I'd render a lot of the rough drafts and hoard them in my google drive for speaker checking. I've been told a few times to release this stuff, so this page will be where I do so. Welcome to lost_songs.
